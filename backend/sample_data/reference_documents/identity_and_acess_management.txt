Identity and Access Management

1. Authentication Methods

LearnSphere supports Single Sign-On (SSO) integration using SAML 2.0 and OpenID Connect (OIDC).

Institutions may integrate their existing identity providers to centralize authentication and lifecycle management.

Accounts not using SSO are protected by enforced password complexity requirements.

2. Role-Based Authorization

Role-based permissions allow institutions to define access levels for administrators, instructors, teaching assistants, and students.

Permissions govern access to course creation tools, grading systems, reporting dashboards, and system configuration settings.

Role definitions can be customized to align with institutional requirements.

3. Multi-Factor Authentication

Multi-factor authentication (MFA) is required for LearnSphere internal administrative users with access to production systems.

Institutions may optionally enable MFA for institutional users based on their own security policies.

4. Account Monitoring

Authentication attempts are logged and monitored.

Accounts are temporarily locked after repeated failed login attempts to prevent brute-force attacks.
