LearnSphere Data Retention Policy

1. Scope of Data

LearnSphere stores student account data, including names, email addresses, institutional identifiers, course enrollment details, assignment submissions, grades, and progress tracking information.

The platform does not intentionally collect sensitive personal data beyond what institutions provide.

2. Retention During Active Contract

Student data is retained for the duration of the institution’s active subscription agreement with LearnSphere.

Data remains accessible to authorized institutional administrators during the contract period.

3. Contract Termination

Upon contract termination, student data is deleted within 30 days unless otherwise required by law.

Institutions may request written confirmation of deletion.

Data deletion processes include removal from active databases and scheduled deletion from backup systems following backup retention timelines.

4. Early Deletion Requests

Institutions may request deletion of specific student records prior to contract termination.

Such requests are processed within 15 business days.

5. Log Retention

System logs related to authentication, administrative access, and security monitoring are retained for 12 months.

Logs are used exclusively for operational troubleshooting, monitoring, and security investigations.

6. Aggregated Analytics

Aggregated and anonymized analytics data may be retained for product improvement and reporting purposes.

Such aggregated data does not contain information that can identify individual students.
