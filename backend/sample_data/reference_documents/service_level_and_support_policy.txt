LearnSphere Service Level and Support Policy
1. Service Availability

LearnSphere targets 99.9% monthly uptime for production services, excluding scheduled maintenance windows.

System availability is monitored continuously using automated monitoring tools. In the event of unplanned outages, technical teams initiate incident response procedures as defined in internal documentation.

2. Scheduled Maintenance

Scheduled maintenance activities are performed during predefined maintenance windows to minimize disruption. Institutions are notified in advance of planned maintenance that may impact service availability.

Emergency maintenance may be performed when necessary to address critical security vulnerabilities or operational risks.

3. Support Services

LearnSphere provides technical support to institutional administrators via email-based ticketing.

Standard support hours are Monday through Friday, 9:00 AM to 6:00 PM Eastern Time, excluding public holidays.

Critical production-impacting issues are prioritized for expedited response and escalation to engineering teams.

4. Incident Management

All incidents are logged and categorized based on severity level.

Incident response procedures include identification, containment, remediation, and post-incident review.

Institutions are notified of confirmed security incidents in accordance with contractual notification obligations.

5. Customer Responsibilities

Institutions are responsible for managing user permissions within their configured roles and for maintaining accurate user account information.

Institutions are also responsible for configuring optional security settings, such as enabling multi-factor authentication for institutional users where applicable.
