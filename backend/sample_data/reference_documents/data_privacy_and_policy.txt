1. Data Ownership

Institutions retain ownership of all student and institutional data stored within the LearnSphere platform.

LearnSphere processes student data solely for the purpose of providing contracted educational services.

2. Regulatory Alignment

LearnSphere is designed to support institutional compliance with applicable education data protection regulations, including the Family Educational Rights and Privacy Act (FERPA) in the United States.

The platform provides access controls, audit logging, and data deletion capabilities to support institutional regulatory requirements.

3. Data Access Controls

Access to student records is governed by role-based permissions as described in the Identity and Access Management documentation.

Institutions determine which administrators, instructors, and staff members are authorized to access specific student information.

4. Data Processing and Subprocessors

LearnSphere utilizes approved subprocessors for infrastructure hosting and related services as described in the Compliance and Vendor Management document.

Subprocessors process student data only under documented contractual obligations.

5. Data Subject Requests

Institutions are responsible for responding to student requests related to access, correction, or deletion of academic records.

LearnSphere provides administrative tools that enable institutions to export or delete student data in accordance with institutional policies.
