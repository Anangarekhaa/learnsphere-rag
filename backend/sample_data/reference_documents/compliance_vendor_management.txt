Compliance and Vendor Management

1. Compliance Alignment

LearnSphere aligns its security controls with SOC 2 Type II requirements.

Internal policies are mapped to ISO 27001 best practice standards to guide risk assessment and control implementation.

Periodic internal reviews assess alignment with documented controls.

2. Employee Screening

Background checks are conducted prior to hiring full-time employees.

Contractors with access to sensitive systems undergo equivalent screening procedures.

Employees are required to sign confidentiality agreements.

3. Vendor Risk Management

LearnSphere uses third-party subprocessors for services such as cloud hosting, infrastructure monitoring, and email delivery.

All subprocessors undergo documented security due diligence prior to engagement.

Subprocessors are contractually obligated to maintain appropriate security safeguards.

4. Ongoing Vendor Oversight

Vendor risk assessments are reviewed annually.

Security documentation from subprocessors is periodically evaluated.

Incident notification obligations are defined in vendor agreements.
